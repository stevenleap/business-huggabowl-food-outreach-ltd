Our Mission

Huggabowl Food Outreach Ltd. is a Calgary-based nonprofit dedicated to addressing food insecurity through community-driven meal programs. We believe that a shared meal can build connections and strengthen neighborhoods.

  • Rooted in Calgary since our founding.
  • Focused on dignity and respect for all.
  • Community-powered volunteer network.
  • Transparent operations and clear mission.

Serving the Calgary area, with a focus on the northwest communities.
